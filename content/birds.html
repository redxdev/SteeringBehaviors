Going Further

This demo shows off flocking with gravity and the y-axis lock turned off. The prefab agent is /SteeringBehaviors/Prefabs/bird, and the example scene is /SteeringBehaviors/Scenes/birds.

This concludes the behavior and framework tutorials. This is a demo that uses the previous behaviors in new and interesting ways.

Birds/Antigravity Flocking

This demo gets a little... weird? It is the same as the original flocking demo, but the gravity on each agent is turned off and the steering manager allows for force to be applied in the y direction. As a result we get agents flocking in mid-air.

Other than just looking strange (mostly due to the model choice), it could also be useful in creating simulations of birds. To take this further, one could add agents on the ground that get attacked (using seek) by the birds, and then within the bird flocks themselves they could try to steal from each other.
